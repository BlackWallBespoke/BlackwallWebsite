Fall Materials

Leather Beyond

The falls determine everything about sensation. Thickness, cut, species, and tanning method all contribute. We source 100% full-grain premium-grade hides, inspected by hand before any cut is made. Pricing for all leathers varies with market conditions at time of order.

Domestic

Cowhide
The benchmark. Predictable, durable. The staple for most floggers.
Suede
Softer surface on both sides of the fall. Thuddy, warm sensation.
Kangaroo
Remarkably strong for its weight. Fine, even grain. A premium choice for speed floggers.
Elk
Supple and heavy. Excellent drape and a good thud. Perfect for warmup flogging.
Deer
Soft, pliable. Light sensation. Good for beginners.
Moose
Large, coarse-grained. Heavy falls with significant thud.
Bison
Rougher texture. Amongst the heaviest leather. Excellent for a booming sound profile.
Buffalo
Open grain, very tough. Substantial impact.
Horse
Tight, fine grain. Excellent strength. A traditional choice.
Bull
Dense and heavy. Maximum impact potential.
Lamb
Extremely soft. Lightest sensation. Luxuriously fine.
Sheep
Softer than cowhide. Gentle, consistent sensation.

Exotic

Ostrich
Distinctive quill pattern. Exotic and tactile.
Stingray
Armoured surface. Unique texture against skin.
Alligator
Scute-patterned. Assertive texture. Extraordinary piece.
Python / Snake
Scale texture. Unique sensory experience.
Eel
Smooth, fine-scaled. Supple and distinctive.
Kodiak
Heavy, dense. Maximum weight falls.
Beaver Tail
Scaly skin texture. Unusual and tactile.
Fish Scale
Delicate surface pattern. Visually stunning.

Fibres

Beyond Leather

Not every commission calls for hide. Natural fibres, synthetic cords, and metal falls each carry their own sensory signature — distinct weight, texture, and sound. Chosen with the same rigour as every other element.

Jute Rope
Soft and forgiving. Thuddy, warm sensation. The benchmark of natural fibre options.
Cotton Rope
Softer and smoother than jute. Gentle, consistent sensation with a clean, natural drape.
Latex
Smooth, fast-moving, and precise. Cool against skin, with a sharp, immediate sting.
Horsehair
Traditional and precise. A distinct whisper against skin.
Kevlar
Extraordinarily strong. Fine, consistent falls with a cutting bite. Not for the uninitiated.
Nylon
Smooth and fast-moving. Precise, stinging sensation.
Paracord
Resilient and consistent. Stingier than natural fibres. 
Metal Falls
Steel, brass, or copper. Cold weight and sound. An instrument of extraordinary presence.
Satin
Smooth, cool, and whisper-light. Available as rope or braided strips — striking to see, remarkable to feel.
Silk
The most luxurious option. Extraordinary softness and drape. Available as rope or braided strips — an aesthetic statement as much as a sensation.
Biothane
Waterproof, easy to clean, and extraordinarily durable. Behaves like leather with none of its maintenance demands.
Rubber
Dense and unforgiving. Heavy thud with a sharp bite at the tip. A very different sensation from any other organic material.

Sensation Profile Index

Creating a sensation profile can be a complex endeavour- finding the right balance of thud, sting, and pain are essential to creating the best piece possible.
 Ratings are relative to the full range of materials in this catalogue — not against an absolute standard.
A 5 in any category is the most intense available here.
Ratings assume standard fall length and cut, standard swing weight, and experienced technique.
Actual sensation varies with build and use.
