Handle Materials

Metal

CNC-machined and lathe-turned from solid stock. Metal handles offer a fundamentally different weight distribution and aesthetic than wood — cold, precise, and permanent. Each is finished to specification and paired with hardware to match.

NOTE: Due to volatility in raw material pricing, final price will be confirmed at time of initial deposit. The $1,000 base price should be considered to be a “jumping off” point for understanding how costly and time-consuming this process is. Manufacturing large numbers of the same item is inexpensive; creating a one-of-one piece that is uniquely yours is not.

Available Metals

Steel
Polished or matte finish · Maximum weight · The most demanding handle available
Brass
Warm gold tone · Can be engraved · Ages beautifully with use
Copper
Living finish · Develops a unique patina over time · Distinctive warmth
Aluminum
Lightweight · Anodised colour options · Contemporary profile
Carbon Fibre
Exceptionally light · Extraordinary visual contrast · Modern and uncompromising
Bronze
Rich warm tone · Denser than brass · Develops a deep, complex patina with age

Metal handles are priced individually based on complexity, finish, and material. Discussed during commission.
