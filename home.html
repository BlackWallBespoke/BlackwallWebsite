The flogger has existed across centuries: first as tool, then as instrument of sexual dominance, and now as functional art. We honour that tradition with our work.

The creation of a Blackwall Flogger is a creative partnership between Artisan and Client. From the selection of a rare wood blank for a hand-turned handle to the final treatment of each leather fall, every decision is made in dialogue with the person who will wield it.

The result is not a product. It is a piece of functional art.

The Process

How a Blackwall piece
comes into being

No catalogues. No existing stock. No off-the-shelf options.
Each piece begins with a conversation, and yields an item never seen before, nor again.
A truly bespoke item. One-of-one. Uniquely yours.

01
The Conversation
You reach out by email. We discuss your vision in minute detail — the weight, the feel, the wood, the leather, the sensation. There is no rush. This step takes as long as it takes.
02
The Order Blank
Once the design is settled, a detailed order blank is completed and the deposit issued. You receive a unique 5-digit order number and QR code — your key to every stage of creation.
03
The Making
Your piece is hand-built to your specifications by one person. Utterly hand-made at every step. No expense spared. No corners cut. Follow progress in real time via your private order page.
04
Delivery
When complete, final payment is due. Your piece ships in a plain unlabelled box. No names attached, no unnecessary records. Discretion is our foundation.

Discretion
