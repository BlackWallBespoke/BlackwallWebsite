The Process

How a Blackwall piece
comes into being

No catalogues. No existing stock. No off-the-shelf options.
 Each piece begins with a conversation, and yields an item never seen before, nor again.
A truly bespoke item. One-of-one. Uniquely yours.

01
The Conversation
You reach out by email.

We discuss your vision in minute detail.

The weight, the feel, the wood, the leather, the sensation. There is no rush. This step takes as long as it takes.
02
The Order Blank
Once the design is settled, a detailed order blank is completed. The deposit is issued.

You receive a unique 5-digit order number and QR code.
This number is your key to every stage of creation.
03
The Making
Your piece is hand-built to your specifications.

By one person. Utterly and completely hand-made, at every step.

No expense spared. No corners cut.

Follow progress in real time via your private order page.
04
Delivery
When complete, final payment is due.

Your piece ships in a plain unlabelled box.

No names attached, no unnecessary records.

Discretion is our foundation.
