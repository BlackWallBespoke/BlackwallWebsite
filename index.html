The Process

How a Blackwall piece
comes into being

No catalogues.
No existing stock.
No off-the-shelf options.
 Each piece begins with a conversation, and yields an item never seen before, nor again.
A truely bespoke item. One-of-one.
Uniquely yours.
We can even fit it for your hand.

01
The Conversation
You reach out by email.

We discuss your vision.

In minute detail.

The weight, the feel, the wood, the leather, the sensation. There is no rush. This step takes as long as it takes.
02
The Order Blank
Once the design is settled, a detailed order blank is completed. The deposit is issued.

You receive a unique 5-digit order number. and QR code.
This number is your key to every stage of creation.   Our options and enhancements allow you to be as active or passive in the creation of your flogger as your like. 
03
The Making
Your piece is hand-built to your specifications.

 By one person.

Utterly and completely hand-made, at every step.

No expense spared.

No corners cut.

Your piece is done when it is done. The process takes hours; sometimes days. Follow progress in real time via your private order page accessible by QR code.
04
Delivery
When complete, final payment is due.   Your piece ships with care in a plain unlabelled cardboard box.

What is inside the box depends on you.
No names attached, no unnecessary records. Discretion is our foundation.
