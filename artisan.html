The Hand Behind the Work

J. Christophe
Aishe

Artisan - Maker - Craftsman

The hand behind the work is J. Christophe Aishe, a master turner with more than three decades of experience. Taught how to turn wood as a child by his grandfather, he has spent almost a decade making bespoke heirloom-grade floggers for discerning private clients, growing his reputation slowly through word-of-mouth and a reputation for the higest quality craftsmanship in a market both saturated with hobby work and devoid of heirloom-quality pieces.

If you look carefully, you'll notice his work in some of the most well-known dungeons on the planet.

Reclusive and introverted, Mr. Aishe is known as much for his eccentrcities as he is for his intense focus and dedication to perfection, and he is famous- perhaps infamous- for spending hours perfecting his pieces, ensuring there are no blemishes or imperfections beyond those conveyed by nature.

His pieces are highly sought after by collectors in discerning circles, and his methodology for creating the "ultra-bespoke" flogger biomechanically measured and built specifically for a client's hand and swinging motion is still the only process of its kind in the world.

For the first time, Mr. Aishe has decided to make his creations available to the commercial public, and has created this website for the purpose of making his pieces more accessible. 
As he puts it, "Spending $1200 on a flogger is perhaps no longer the domain of the rich seeking pieces of status, but rather also the connoisseur of Kink, and the serious practitioner of the craft."
