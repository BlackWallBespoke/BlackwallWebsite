Selection

Everything chosen
with purpose

This is not a menu- it is a conversation about two things: Use Profile and Aesthetic Profile.

In both of those profiles, each selection a client makes interacts and creates and effect on the other choices, and they all come together to create a use profile for the piece.

The weight of the wood shapes the profile of the falls: Heavier woods require heavier falls, or the handle will not balance correctly. The length determines the use profile; the fall thickness determines the sound and the sensation. These choices are made together, never in isolation.
,
What wood you use provides the foundation for the aesthetics of your piece- it dictates what colours you can use for your piece- Purple Heart wood and bright yellow falls looks cartoonish, while pink or purple looks refined, Imperial even. The image- the message- that you want your piece to convey is chosen with your aesthetic profile.

Pricing varies by material. Exotics and rare woods carry premiums that reflect their sourcing difficulty, scarcity, and the additional skill required to work them properly. All pricing is confirmed during the commission conversation.

Fall Materials

Leather Beyond

The falls determine everything about sensation. Thickness, cut, species, and tanning method all contribute. We source 100% full-grain premium-grade hides, inspected by hand before any cut is made. Pricing for all leathers varies with market conditions at time of order.

Cowhide
The benchmark. Predictable, durable. The staple for most floggers.
Suede
Softer surface on both sides of the fall. Thuddy, warm sensation.
Kangaroo
Remarkably strong for its weight. Fine, even grain. A premium choice for speed floggers.
Elk
Supple and heavy. Excellent drape and a good thud. Perfect for warmup flogging
Deer
Soft, pliable. Light sensation. Good for beginners.
Moose
Large, coarse-grained. Heavy falls with significant thud.
Bison
Rougher texture. Amongst the heaviest leather. Excellent for a booming sound profile.
Buffalo
Open grain, very tough. Substantial impact.
Horse
Tight, fine grain. Excellent strength. A traditional choice.
Bull
Dense and heavy. Maximum impact potential.
Lamb
Extremely soft. Lightest sensation. Luxuriously fine.
Sheep
Softer than cowhide. Gentle, consistent sensation.
Ostrich
Distinctive quill pattern. Exotic and tactile.
Stingray
Armoured surface. Unique texture against skin.
Alligator
Scute-patterned. Assertive texture. Extraordinary piece.
Python / Snake
Scale texture. Unique sensory experience.
Eel
Smooth, fine-scaled. Supple and distinctive.
Kodiak
Heavy, dense. Maximum weight falls.
Beaver Tail
Scaly skin texture. Unusual and tactile.
Fish Scale
Delicate surface pattern. Visually stunning.

Non-leather options also available: braided rope, latex, horsehair, kevlar, nylon, paracord, and metal falls. Discussed during commission.
