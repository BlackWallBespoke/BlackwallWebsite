Selection

Everything chosen
with purpose

This is not a menu — it is a conversation about two things: Use Profile and Aesthetic Profile.

In both of those profiles, each selection a client makes interacts and creates an effect on the other choices, and they all come together to create a use profile for the piece.

The weight of the wood shapes the profile of the falls: heavier woods require heavier falls, or the handle will not balance correctly. The length determines the use profile; the fall thickness determines the sound and the sensation. These choices are made together, never in isolation.

What wood you use provides the foundation for the aesthetics of your piece — it dictates what colours you can use. Purple Heart wood and bright yellow falls looks cartoonish, while pink or purple looks refined, imperial even. The image — the message — that you want your piece to convey is chosen with your aesthetic profile.

Pricing varies by material. Exotics and rare woods carry premiums that reflect their sourcing difficulty, scarcity, and the additional skill required to work them properly. All pricing is confirmed during the commission conversation.
