Step One

Enhancements

Structural and material enhancements that alter the character of the piece itself.

1
Enhancement
Laser Cutting — $125
Precision laser-cut falls with clean, exact edges and the option for intricate patterning along the length. Produces a distinct visual profile and a subtly different sensation at the tip. Available on leather and select synthetic falls.
Discussed during commission · Subject to material compatibility
2
Enhancement
Latex / PVC Falls Coating — $150
A smooth, sealed latex or PVC coating applied to the falls. Changes both the aesthetic and the sensation profile — glossy, cool to the touch, and with a crisper impact character. Easy to clean and maintain.
Discussed during commission · Subject to material compatibility
3
Enhancement · Fall Colouring
Two-Colour Falls — +$50
Falls cut from two different dye colours of the same leather type, mixed within the same fall set. The contrast can be subtle — two tones of the same family — or deliberate. The material character and sensation profile remains consistent; only the visual changes. Colour pairing discussed and confirmed during commission.
Same leather type throughout · Colour pairing confirmed during commission · Available on most leather falls
4
Enhancement · Fall Colouring
Three-Colour Falls — +$100
Three dye colours of the same leather type distributed across the fall set. The effect can range from a graduated triadic blend to a structured pattern of intentional contrast. More complex to source and cut than a two-colour set, and the distribution of each colour is discussed before cutting begins.
Same leather type throughout · Distribution and proportion confirmed before cutting · Longer sourcing lead time may apply
5
Enhancement · Fall Colouring
Four-Plus Colour Falls — +$150
Four or more dye colours within a single fall set — all from the same leather type. At this range, the fall set becomes as much a visual statement as a functional one. Sourcing four or more distinct dye lots of the same species and tanning process takes time; this enhancement requires patience and a clear palette conversation upfront. The result justifies both.
Same leather type throughout · Palette conversation required before work begins · Extended sourcing lead time expected
6
Enhancement · Fall Construction
Mixed Fall Types — From +$200
Falls drawn from two or more different materials within the same set — combining, for example, cowhide and kangaroo, or suede and latex, or elk and horsehair. Each combination produces a sensation profile that no single material achieves alone: the thud of one layered against the sting of another, distributed across the same strike. Not all material pairings are compatible or advisable; the combination is discussed and assessed during commission. Pricing reflects the complexity of sourcing and construction.
Compatibility of materials confirmed before commission · Sensation profile discussed in detail · Pricing confirmed per specific combination
7
Enhancement · Fall Construction
Fall Braiding — +$150
Individual falls braided along part or all of their length before the final cut. Braiding adds weight, changes the drape, and alters the sensation profile — a braided fall lands with more focus and authority than a flat one of the same width. It also changes the visual character of the set considerably: braided falls have a rope-like texture that reads as deliberate and constructed. Works best on leathers with sufficient thickness and pliability — cowhide, kangaroo, elk, and horse are the most suitable. The braid pattern and length are discussed during commission.
Not available on all leather types · Braid pattern and extent confirmed during commission · Adds weight and alters sensation profile
8
Enhancement · Fall Construction
Fall Accoutrements — From +$100
Additions to the falls themselves — beyond the material and the cut. This includes tipping the ends of falls with metal, cord, or knotwork; adding small rings or charms threaded onto individual falls; weighting the fall tips with wrapped lead or brass; or finishing the fall ends with decorative whipping in contrasting thread or leather lace. Each addition changes both the appearance and the landing character of the fall. Some accoutrements are purely aesthetic; others alter the sensation profile meaningfully. The specific additions, their placement, and their effect are discussed during commission. Pricing depends on the complexity and number of accoutrements applied.
Discussed and designed during commission · Some options affect sensation profile · Pricing confirmed per specific configuration
